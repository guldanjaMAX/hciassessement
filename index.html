Common Questions

What You Need to Know

How long does the assessment take?
Under 3 minutes. There are 10 questions, each asking you to rate a statement on a 1 to 5 scale. Most people complete it in about 2 minutes.
Is it really free?
Yes, completely free. No credit card, no trial period, no hidden fees. You will receive your score immediately after completing the assessment. We will also email you a PDF summary of your results.
Who sees my results?
Only you. Your results are private and sent directly to the email address you provide. We do not share individual assessment data with anyone. Aggregate, anonymized data may be used in published research.
What happens after I get my score?
You will receive your score card immediately, along with an email with your full results PDF. Over the following two weeks, we will send you additional context about your score, including tips for the dimensions where you scored lowest. If you want to go deeper, you can schedule a complimentary Conflict Diagnostic with Megan Hunter.
What's the difference between this and the full diagnostic?
This free assessment gives you a high level snapshot across 10 questions. The full Conflict Readiness Diagnostic is a comprehensive 40 question assessment across all 8 dimensions, administered to multiple stakeholders, and includes a detailed report with benchmarks and specific recommendations. The diagnostic results are presented by Megan in a personal 30 minute debrief.
Can I use this for my whole team?
The free version is designed for a single HR leader or senior manager. If you want to assess your broader leadership team or multiple departments, the full Conflict Readiness Diagnostic is designed exactly for that, with stakeholder comparison and alignment analysis.
